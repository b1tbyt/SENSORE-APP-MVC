?? REAL-TIME DATA FLOW

  Every 100ms:

    PressureDataService (generates data)
            ?
    OnHeatmapUpdated (event fires)
            ?
    HeatmapBroadcastService (listens & converts)
            ?
    SignalR (broadcasts to clients)
            ?
    JavaScript (receives update)
            ?
    Browser (renders instantly)
